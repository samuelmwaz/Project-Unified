Core Service Pillars

Discover our integrated platform that transforms public transport into a foundation for digital trust, employment access, and economic formalization across Zambia.

AutoRelief

Mobility

QR-based digital ticketing, closed-loop wallet payments, and access to verified mechanics and maintenance services. Reducing cash handling risks while building digital trust.

The Job Connect

Employment

Guaranteed interviews, documented testing processes, and tiered packages for both employees and employers. Connecting skilled workers with verified opportunities.

Digital Wallet

Payments

Secure, closed-loop digital wallet supporting all platform transactions with integration to MTN, Airtel Money, and Zamtel. USSD access for non-smartphone users.

Vendor Regulation

Trust

Comprehensive verification and management of vendors across key sectors including mechanics, fuel delivery, and professional services. Building trust through transparency.

Our Vision

Building Digital Trust Through Daily Use

We envision a Zambia where public transport serves as the gateway to digital inclusion, economic opportunity, and verified professional networks. Project Unified addresses the inefficiencies of cash-based systems and informal trust networks by creating a practical, scalable digital ecosystem.
